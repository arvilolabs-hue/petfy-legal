Petfy · Account Deletion

Delete Account and Data

You can request deletion of your account and related data without reopening the app.

Last updated: June 2026 · Contact: legal@petfy.app

What gets deleted

  • User account.
  • Profile and associated preferences.
  • Pet data, health data, reminders, and account-linked content.
  • Associated private files when applicable.

What may be retained

We may retain certain information only if required by law, security, fraud prevention, or regulatory compliance. If that happens, it will be described in the privacy policy.

How to request deletion

1
Open the in-app flow

Go to Settings > Security > Delete account and data.

2
Confirm your identity

The app may ask for verification to prevent accidental or unauthorized deletion.

3
If you cannot access the app

Email legal@petfy.app with the subject “Delete my Petfy account”.

Google Play requires this page to be functional and let users start the deletion request without reinstalling the app.

Response time

We aim to complete or respond to deletion requests within a reasonable time. If additional steps are required, they will be clearly explained.

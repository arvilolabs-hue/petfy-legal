Delete Account and Data

You can request deletion of your account and related data without reopening the app.

Eliminar cuenta y datos

Puedes solicitar la eliminacion de tu cuenta y datos relacionados sin volver a abrir la app.

Last updated: June 2026Arvilo

What gets deleted

  • User account, profile, and preferences.
  • Pet data, health data, reminders, and account-linked content.
  • Associated private files when applicable.

Que se elimina

  • Cuenta, perfil y preferencias del usuario.
  • Datos de mascotas, salud, recordatorios y contenido vinculado a la cuenta.
  • Archivos privados asociados cuando aplique.

How to request deletion

1
Open the in-app flow

Go to Settings > Security > Delete account and data.

2
Confirm your identity

The app may ask for verification to prevent accidental or unauthorized deletion.

3
If you cannot access the app

Email arvilolabs@gmail.com with the subject "Delete my Petfy account".

Certain data may be retained only when required by law, security, fraud prevention, or regulatory compliance.

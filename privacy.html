Privacy Policy

This policy explains what information Petfy handles, why it is used, and how users can control their data.

1. Data controller

Petfy is operated by Arvilo Labs. The contact email for privacy and support matters is legal@petfy.app.

This policy applies to the Petfy mobile app and its associated web services.

2. Data we process

Data type Examples Purpose
Account data Email, user ID, session, language preferences, and app settings. Authentication, access, and personalization.
Pet data Name, species, breed, sex, birth date, photo, and notes. Display profiles and organize care.
Health and care data Vaccines, medication, reminders, weight, feeding, symptoms, and observations. Track pet care history and alerts.
User content Photos, documents, support messages, and text entered in the app. App functionality and support.
Location Approximate or precise location when searching for nearby vets. Show nearby results and calculate distance.
Purchases Subscription status and entitlements managed by the payment provider. Enable premium features.
Diagnostics and security Errors, technical events, logs, and telemetry. Stability, security, and debugging.

3. How we use data

  • Create and maintain user accounts.
  • Store pet profiles, reminders, and health records.
  • Show nearby veterinarians, maps, distance, and availability.
  • Send local notifications and manage reminders.
  • Provide AI features with consent and within an informational scope.
  • Handle support, security, error recovery, and subscriptions.
Petfy does not use this information for personalized advertising.

4. Third parties and providers

Petfy may use external providers only to deliver necessary technical services.

  • Supabase: authentication, database, storage, and serverless functions.
  • Google Places / Maps: nearby veterinarian search.
  • OpenAI: informational AI assistance.
  • RevenueCat: subscriptions, when enabled.
  • Sentry: error diagnostics, when enabled.
  • Expo: build and notification services, depending on configuration.

These providers act as processors or technical service providers depending on the context. Petfy does not sell your personal information to advertisers.

5. Data retention

We keep information while the account remains active or as long as necessary to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, prevent fraud, or enforce our terms.

When a user requests deletion, we follow the app and web deletion flow, subject to any data that must be retained by law or for legitimate security purposes.

6. User rights

  • Review, correct, or update account and pet information.
  • Request account and data deletion.
  • Disable device permissions where possible.
  • Withdraw consent from features that depend on it.

Privacy requests can be sent to legal@petfy.app.

7. Security

We use access controls, authentication, server-side policies, and reasonable safeguards to protect information. No system is perfectly secure, but Petfy works to reduce unnecessary exposure and protect user data.

  • We do not expose secrets in the visible interface.
  • Access to data is restricted to authenticated users and permitted server logic.
  • Photos and documents are treated as sensitive user information.

8. Contact

Privacy and support email: legal@petfy.app

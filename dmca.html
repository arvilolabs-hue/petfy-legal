Copyright and Infringement Reporting

Petfy respects intellectual property. If you believe content hosted or published through the platform infringes your rights, you can send a report.

Last updated: June 2026 · Contact: legal@petfy.app

What to include

  • Your full name and contact details.
  • Clear identification of the protected work.
  • The exact location of the allegedly infringing content.
  • A good-faith statement that the use is not authorized.
  • A statement under penalty of perjury that the information is accurate.
  • A physical or electronic signature.

Counter-notification

If your content was removed by mistake or misidentification, you may reply with a counter-notification and explain why you believe no infringement exists.
